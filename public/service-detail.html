Headlight Retrofit

Starting at ₱18,000 3 – 5 Days

Upgrade your factory headlights with high-performance projector or bi-LED retrofit assemblies. Our certified technicians meticulously disassemble, retrofit, reseal, and re-aim your headlights for a cleaner, brighter beam pattern — dramatically improving night-time visibility and giving your vehicle a distinctive, modern aesthetic without sacrificing OEM fitment quality.

Key Features & Benefits

  • Up to 3× brighter output versus factory halogen bulbs
  • Precise HID or bi-LED projector for a sharp, focused cutoff line
  • OEM-quality re-sealing with butyl glue — weatherproof & long-lasting
  • Professional beam aiming included — no blinding oncoming traffic
  • Available with DRL halos, sequential turn signals, and custom shrouds
  • 1-year workmanship warranty on all retrofit installations
